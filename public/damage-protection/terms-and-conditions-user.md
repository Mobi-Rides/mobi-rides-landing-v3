# MobiRides Damage Protection Terms (User Summary)

_Last updated: 2026-04-02_

This summary explains how Damage Protection works for renters and hosts on MobiRides.

## 1) What Damage Protection Covers
Paid tiers include coverage for:
- Accidental collision damage
- Single-vehicle incidents
- Weather damage (hail, flooding, storm)
- Vandalism and malicious damage
- Attempted theft damage

## 2) What Is Not Covered
Damage Protection does not cover:
- Intentional or reckless damage
- Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs
- Unlicensed or unauthorized drivers
- Off-road use (unless the vehicle is rated for off-road)
- Mechanical wear and tear
- Pre-existing damage documented at handover
- Damage during illegal activities
- Consequential losses (such as loss of income or travel delays)
- Third-party liability

## 3) Coverage Tiers
### No Coverage
- Premium: P0/day
- Coverage cap: None
- Renter liability: 100%

### Basic
- Premium: P80/day
- Coverage cap: P8,000
- Renter excess: 20% of approved claim

### Standard
- Premium: P150/day
- Coverage cap: P20,000
- Renter excess: 15% of approved claim

### Premium
- Premium: P250/day
- Coverage cap: P50,000
- Renter excess: 10% of approved claim

## 4) How Claims Work
1. Report the incident in the app within 24 hours.
2. Initial review is completed within 48 hours.
3. Additional evidence may be requested within 48 hours.
4. Provide any requested information within 7 days.
5. Final adjudication is completed within 5 business days once the file is complete.
6. Approved payouts are processed within 24 hours.

## 5) Auto-Approval and Fees
- Claims of P500 or less may be auto-approved when evidence is sufficient.
- A fixed P150 admin fee is deducted from each approved payout.

## 6) Example Excess Calculation
For an approved claim amount:
- Basic: renter pays 20% + payout admin fee applies
- Standard: renter pays 15% + payout admin fee applies
- Premium: renter pays 10% + payout admin fee applies

## 7) Important Notes
- Damage Protection applies per booking.
- Terms may be updated from time to time.
- Final claim outcomes depend on submitted evidence and policy eligibility.

For questions or help with claims, contact MobiRides support through the app.
