## üìê Technical Specifications

### Screenshot Standards (1170x2532px - 9:16 Ratio)
- **Device:** iPhone 13/14 or equivalent Android
- **Resolution:** 1170 x 2532 px (portrait, 9:16 aspect ratio)
- **Format:** PNG (clarity priority) or JPG (file size priority)
- **Status Bar:** Full battery, good signal, 9:41 AM time
- **UI Cleanup:** Remove personal info, use demo accounts
- **Editing:** Figma, Canva, or Adobe Express
- **Annotations:** Use arrows/circles to highlight features
- **Phone Frame:** Add subtle mock-up border for social posts

### Video Recording Specs (9:16 Format)
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum (1920 x 1080)
- **Frame Rate:** 30 fps or 60 fps
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Orientation:** Portrait for Reels/Stories, Square for Feed
- **Length:** Reels 15-60s, Feed 30-90s
- **Tools:** iOS Screen Recording, CapCut, InShot
- **Flow:** Smooth actions, 2s pause per screen, 1.5x speed edit

- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (vertical)

## üìà PERFORMANCE TRACKING

### Engagement Metrics to Track:
- **Reach:** How many people saw the post
- **Impressions:** Total views (including repeats)
- **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Reach
- **Click-Through Rate:** Link clicks / Reach
- **Save Rate:** Saves / Reach (high value signal)
- **Share Rate:** Shares / Reach (virality indicator)

### Top-Performing Content Analysis:
- Which modules get the most engagement?
- Renter vs Host content performance?
- Screenshot vs Video performance?
- Best posting times?
- Most effective CTAs?

### Weekly Review:
- Top 3 posts by reach
- Top 3 posts by engagement rate
- Lowest performers (why?)
- Adjustments for next week
